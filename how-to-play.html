How to Play Ultimate Tic Tac

Go Back

In your first turn you can play anywhere you want. After that, you can only play inside of the white sqare which place is determined by your opponent's move. If the white sqaure isn't visible, you can play anywhere you want. This can mean 2 things:

  1. This is the first turn of the game.
  2. You or your opponent sent the other player to a won board.
